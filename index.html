I bet most of you have no idea what day of the week you were born on. Ghanians have a beautiful culture where they give their children first names as a 'day name'which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. The names vary with gender, having different names for males and females. These are called Akan Names.
Here are the Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama
