About

Have you ever wondered what day of the week you were born on?

I bet most of you have no idea what day of the week you were born on. I guess thats why you're here to find out.
Ghanians have a beautiful culture where they give their children are given their first name as a 'day name'
which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
They names vary with gender, having different names for males and females. These are called Akan Names.
Here are the Ghanian day names.

Male

  • Sunday: Kwasi
  • Monday: Kwadwo
  • Tuesday: Kwabena
  • Wednesday: Kwaku
  • Thursday: Yaw
  • Friday: Kofi
  • Saturday: Kwame

Female

  • Sunday: Akosua
  • Monday: Adwoa
  • Tuesday: Abenaa
  • Wednesday: Akua
  • Thursday: Yaa
  • Friday: Afua
  • Saturday: Ama

Beatiful names aren't they? I'm sure you're curiuos to find out your Akan name, or that of a loved one.
